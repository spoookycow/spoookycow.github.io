Colder Waters

Surprisingly there is a lot of life in the arctic waters. Larger predators like Orca live among these waters and Narwhal can also be seen. The Orca are mighty hunters and aggressive towards most they encounter. Local fisherman often report angry Orca capsizing their boats or working together to steal fish.
Narwhall are much kinder and will often bring gifts to those that visit the sea often. They use seaweed and algae to fashion fancy costumes and ornaments for their long tusks.
Some popular psychics and marine biologists have reported telepathic communication with elder Narwhals. Little is known aboout these conversations but Narwhals are rumored to gossip a lot.

A lesser known creature is the Sea Angel which is a tiny but fierce little animal. (seen below)
Don't let their cute name and innocent appearance fool you. These small creatures are truly evil. They use their charm to entice their prey before quickly and efficiently gobbling them up whole.
