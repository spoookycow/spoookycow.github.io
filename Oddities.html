Strange Oddities

There have been
certain reports...
of strange sightings at lower depths. Sightings that seem to suggest there may be some truth to the old fairytales and myths about the ocean.

These reports come from allover the world. Most accounts suggest that these creatures are friendly however they are carnivorous. They seem to form bonds with dolphins, sharks, and other larger marine life.

Other Species

Here is a collection of sketches by an avid merfolk scholar, artist, and historian. As you can see there are many different species with many differnt features that define them.
For example some do not have regular fins but instead tentacles or extra appendages. It would appear that their appearance take on the characteristics and sizes of the fish they are most closely linked to. It can be seen that some arctic mermaid exhibit behavior and even a tusk similar to the Narhwal.
It can also be noted that not all mermaid are friendly and some are even carnivorous, showing cannibalistic tendencies.
